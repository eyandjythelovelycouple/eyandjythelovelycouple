Hello! My name is Jaeyoung Lim, a Masters student studying robotics at ETH Zurich. I am currently working as a intern in Auterion AG working on opensource autopilot PX4 for commerical applications.

I am interested in autonomous systems, especially in aerial robotics. I have worked on multirotor systems as a number of projects for research as well as a hobbyist. I am fascinated in the control / planning / perception of dynamic systems that work in the real world. My personal projects can be seen at the 404warehouse.net blog

I recieved B.S. in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Seoul National University.
